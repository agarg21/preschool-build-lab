## Overall Verdict

The activity pack is structurally well-designed, with quick starts, clear timelines, and stop cues that lower parent friction. The remaining issues are mechanical edge cases that could frustrate a real child: folding bridge paper the wrong way, reshaping a waterlogged foil boat, and putting parent actions inside read-aloud kid steps.

## Scores

- Parent followability: 7.8/10
- Kid engagement: 8.0/10
- Originality/usefulness: 7.5/10
- SEO quality/content depth: 8.5/10

## Remaining Blockers Before Testing

- Parent actions appear inside some read-aloud kid-step lines.
- Bridge folding needs to say lengthwise or accordion-style so the bridge still spans the gap.
- The foil boat rescue needs to say to remove the boat and dump the water before reshaping.
- Shadow Builder should place the flashlight on a stable surface so moving the toy works consistently.

## Accepted Edits

1. Replace "road cover" with a more concrete surface phrase.
2. Specify folding the bridge paper lengthwise like an accordion.
3. Set the flashlight on a stable surface for Shadow Builder.
4. Use "move" instead of "slide" for shadow steps.
5. Rewrite Windproof Tower's parent action as a kid-facing wind cue.
6. Rewrite the boat's first step as a child-facing action.
7. Add the remove/dump/reshape rescue step for a sunken foil boat.
