# Original STEM for 4 Year Olds - Parent Review Fixes

Date: 2026-06-30
Page: `site/collections/original-stem-activities-for-4-year-olds.html`

## Trigger

Manual parent review raised three issues:

1. Ramp Detective used towel/foil, but foil felt questionable for friction difference and unnecessary mess.
2. Bridge Rescue said "two bridge tests" without explaining what that meant.
3. Tiny Boat Cargo Test did not explain how to make the foil boat.

## Changes Made

- Removed foil from Ramp Detective and changed the test surfaces to towel, paper, or placemat.
- Changed the kid-facing ramp phrase from "surface" to "road."
- Replaced "two bridge tests" with "one flat bridge and one stronger bridge."
- Clarified that accordion folding is adult help: "ask me to fold..."
- Added a "Make the boat" mini-section for the foil boat:
  - fold edges up
  - pinch corners
  - keep the bottom wide and mostly flat
- Updated the testing brief materials for Ramp Detective and Bridge Rescue.

## Follow-Up

Run these exact activities with a 4-year-old before turning any of them into standalone polished pages.
