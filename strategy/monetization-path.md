# Monetization Path

## Current Stage

Kid Activity Lab is not ready to optimize for revenue yet. The current stage is trust, indexing, and useful original content.

## Likely Future Paths

Display ads may make sense after meaningful organic traffic exists.

Affiliate links may make sense for household materials, activity supplies, or tested tools, but only when the page remains useful without the link.

Small digital products may make sense later:

- printable activity cards
- themed activity packs
- rainy-day packs
- age-4 STEM testing pack
- low-prep weekly activity plans

## Monetization Rules

- Do not let monetization drive page creation before the content is useful.
- Do not recommend products that have not been reviewed for parent practicality.
- Keep affiliate language clear and honest.
- Ask the user before adding ads, affiliate links, products, email capture, or checkout flows.
