### Avoid For Now

1. Local near-me intent
   - activities for 3 year olds near me
   - indoor activities for kids near me
   - extracurricular activities for 4 year olds near me

   This is a different product: local directory/events.

2. Broad video pages
   - youtube activities for kids
   - youtube movement activities for kids
   - activity videos for preschoolers

   Video curation should support cards, not be the main SEO strategy. Broad YouTube intent is hard because Google/YouTube own the SERP.

3. Medical/special-needs modifiers
   - fine motor activities for kids with autism
   - sensory activities for kids with ADHD
   - sensory activities for kids with pica

   High trust/safety burden. Do not target until we have expert review.

## What To Curate

Curate cards, not articles.

Each page should have:

- 8-12 activities.
- Age fit.
- Setup time.
- Mess level.
- Adult help level.
- Materials.
- 3-4 steps.
- Parent safety note.
- Link to source video or source idea when used.
- Optional printable/card download later.

The content database should tag every activity by:

- age: 3, 4, 5, 6
- context: at-home, indoor, rainy-day, no-prep, screen-free, independent
- skill: fine-motor, gross-motor, STEM, math, literacy, pretend-play, sensory
- material: cardboard, tape, cups, paper rolls, blocks, LEGO/DUPLO, magnetic tiles
- setup time: 1, 2, 5, 10, 15 minutes
- mess: low, medium, high
- adult help: low, medium, high

## Expected Early Impressions

On a fresh domain:

- Conservative: 0-300 impressions in month 1, 300-1,500 in month 2.
- Decent: 1,500-5,000 total impressions by month 2 if indexing is fast and age pages match variants.
- Strong: 5,000+ if Google starts testing printable, educational, STEM, and indoor clusters early.

The first goal is query discovery, not traffic. We want Search Console to show which query families Google thinks the site is relevant for.
