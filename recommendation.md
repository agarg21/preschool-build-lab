## What SERP Validation Found

### Building With Kids

The SERPs are competitive but accessible.

Evidence:

- Small and niche publishers rank repeatedly: Busy Toddler, Toddler Approved, TinkerLab, Frugal Fun 4 Boys, Little Bins for Little Hands, Hands On As We Grow, Preschool Toolkit, Pink Stripey Socks, and similar sites.
- Many ranking posts are old, broad, Pinterest-shaped, classroom-oriented, or not age-specific.
- Exact parent filters are under-served: low mess, no prep, no glue, apartment-friendly, 4-year-old-specific, toy-car testing, tape safety, small-part safety, and what a parent actually needs to know before starting.
- 23 of 25 reviewed queries looked beatable; 2 were maybe.

Best wedge:

> Parent-tested building projects for 3-4 year olds using household materials, with honest notes on age fit, prep time, mess, supervision, safety, and what actually worked.

### JC/NYC Kids Activities

The SERPs are also promising, especially for exact local utility.

Evidence:

- Local publishers and small/local sites rank repeatedly: Hoboken Girl, JCFamilies, Everything Jersey City, ParentsOfJC, NJToddler, NJ Mom, Mommy Poppins, Reddit, official venue pages, and local park pages.
- Broad local activity queries are competitive, but exact logistics queries are weak.
- Strong gaps exist around age fit, bathrooms, shade, stroller access, PATH/transit routing, car-free planning, cost, crowd levels, nap timing, and rainy-day backups.
- 25 of 25 reviewed queries looked beatable if the user can verify details.

Best wedge:

> Parent-tested Jersey City and nearby NYC activities for preschoolers, with logistics that event calendars skip.

Main risk:

- The local niche becomes a maintenance job. It is strongest if it stays evergreen and logistics-focused, not a weekly event calendar.

## Decision

Choose **building-with-kids** for the first publish test.

Why:

- Faster MVP.
- Easier to test at home.
- Less dependent on changing local hours, closures, prices, and event calendars.
- Stronger fit with original photos and firsthand parent notes.
- Can publish 20 pages quickly without needing to travel.

Keep JC/NYC as the second validated path if:

- You are excited to visit/review places.
- You are willing to verify local logistics.
- You want local distribution through parent groups.
