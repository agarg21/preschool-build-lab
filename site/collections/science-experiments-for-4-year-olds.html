Science

Science experiments for 4 year olds.

The strongest science experiments for 4 year olds are short and observable. The child should be able to predict, test, notice, and try again without waiting for a long result.

Quick pick: Pick experiments where the result is easy to see: sinking, floating, sticking, melting, mixing, rolling, or making a sound.

Sink or Float Tray

prediction, sorting, early science talk

4-6 years 5 min medium mess medium help
Needshallow water tray, safe household objects
  1. Pick one object.
  2. Guess sink or float.
  3. Test it.
  4. Sort and try another.

Parent check: Use a shallow tray and stay next to water.

Ice Melt Rescue

temperature, observation, patient testing

4-6 years 8 min medium mess medium help
Needice cube, warm water, spoon, tray
  1. Put ice on a tray.
  2. Touch with a spoon.
  3. Add warm drops.
  4. Watch it melt.

Parent check: Use warm water, not hot water.

Magnet Hunt

sorting, prediction, material testing

4-6 years 5 min low mess medium help
Needlarge magnet, safe objects
  1. Pick an object.
  2. Guess if it sticks.
  3. Try the magnet.
  4. Make two piles.

Parent check: Use one large magnet and avoid tiny magnets or batteries.

Shadow Shape Match

light, shapes, cause and effect

4-6 years 5 min low mess medium help
Needflashlight, blocks, wall
  1. Pick a block.
  2. Shine the light.
  3. Find the shadow.
  4. Move it closer.

Parent check: Do not shine flashlights into eyes.

Color Mixing Cups

prediction, color science, careful pouring

4-6 years 6 min medium mess medium help
Needclear cups, water, food coloring
  1. Fill two cups.
  2. Add two colors.
  3. Pour together.
  4. Name the new color.

Parent check: Use tiny drops and protect the table.

Sound Shaker Match

sound, listening, matching evidence

4-6 years 5 min low mess medium help
Needclosed containers, rice, blocks, pasta
  1. Shake one container.
  2. Guess what is inside.
  3. Shake another.
  4. Match the sounds.

Parent check: Seal containers well and supervise small fillers.

Water Drop Race

surface tension, gentle breath control, observation

4-6 years 5 min medium mess medium help
Needwax paper, water drops, straw
  1. Put drops on wax paper.
  2. Blow gently.
  3. Race two drops.
  4. Try a bigger drop.

Parent check: Use gentle blowing and keep water away from electronics.

Ramp Texture Test

friction, prediction, toy car testing

4-6 years 6 min low mess medium help
Needramp, towel, foil, toy car
  1. Roll on a plain ramp.
  2. Add a towel.
  3. Try foil.
  4. Compare the rolls.

Parent check: Keep the ramp low and the rolling path clear.

How to choose safely

Match the activity to the child in front of you, not just the age label. Use larger pieces when younger siblings are nearby, keep ramps and towers low, and supervise tape, pom poms, small blocks, water, rubber bands, or anything that can go in a mouth.
